## Summary

Guest Reviews and Ratings Platform

## Problem Statement

NovaTrek Adventures lacks a guest reviews capability (CAP-1.7). Guests who complete adventures have no way to share their experience with future guests. This gap affects:

1. **Booking conversion** — adventure tourism buyers rely heavily on peer reviews; without them, guests default to price or brand familiarity
2. **Quality visibility** — operations teams have no aggregated guest satisfaction signal per trip or guide
3. **Content marketing** — no user-generated content pipeline for marketing use
4. **Guide accountability** — no per-guide satisfaction metrics to inform training and assignment decisions

## Proposed Solution

Build a new svc-reviews microservice that supports:

- Star rating (1-5) with optional per-category breakdown (safety, guide quality, value, scenery, difficulty accuracy)
- Written review with title and body
- Reservation-gated submission (only completed adventures)
- Moderation pipeline (automated + manual)
- Aggregated rating summaries per trip and per guide
- Helpful vote mechanism for community curation

## Acceptance Criteria

1. A guest with a COMPLETED reservation can submit a review with a 1-5 star rating and optional text
2. A guest cannot submit more than one review per reservation
3. A guest without a completed reservation receives a clear error
4. Submitted reviews enter PENDING_MODERATION status
5. Moderators can approve or reject reviews from a queue
6. Only APPROVED reviews are visible in public listings
7. The trip catalog displays an aggregated rating summary (average, count, distribution)
8. Reviews support optimistic locking to prevent concurrent edit conflicts
9. Guests can mark reviews as helpful (one vote per guest per review)
10. Guide rating summaries are available per guide

## Business Impact

- Increased booking conversion through social proof on trip pages
- Guest satisfaction signal aggregated per trip and guide
- User-generated content pipeline for marketing
- Guide performance visibility for operations
