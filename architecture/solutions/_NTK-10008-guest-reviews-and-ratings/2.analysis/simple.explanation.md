# Simple Explanation — Guest Reviews and Ratings

## The Problem

When guests finish an amazing glacier hike or canyon rafting trip at NovaTrek, they have no way to tell other guests about their experience. Future guests browsing the trip catalog see the same description for every trip — they cannot tell which adventures are truly outstanding and which might not meet their expectations.

## The Solution

We are building a reviews system where guests can rate their adventure (1 to 5 stars) and write about their experience after their trip. Before a review appears publicly, it goes through a moderation check to keep content appropriate. Each trip page will show an average rating and guest comments, so future guests can make informed choices.

## Why It Matters

Guest reviews are the single strongest driver of booking decisions in adventure tourism. When a trip shows "4.7 stars from 127 reviews," guests trust it. Reviews also help NovaTrek identify which guides deliver exceptional experiences and which trips might need improvement — turning guest feedback into actionable quality insights.
