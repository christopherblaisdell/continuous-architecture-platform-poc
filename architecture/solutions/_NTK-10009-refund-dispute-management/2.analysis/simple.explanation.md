# Simple Explanation — Refund and Dispute Management

## The Problem

When a guest wants a refund — maybe their trip was cancelled due to weather, or they need to cancel for personal reasons — the process today is entirely manual. A guest services agent looks up the booking, decides the refund amount based on their judgment, and processes it. There is no system tracking whether the refund followed company policy, no automatic notifications to the guest about progress, and no structured way to handle complex cases that need a manager's approval.

## The Solution

We are adding a structured refund and dispute workflow to the payments system. When a guest requests a refund, the system automatically checks company policies (like "full refund if cancelled 48 hours before the trip, 50% if within 24 hours"). Simple cases are approved automatically. More complex cases go to an agent's review queue, and high-value disputes escalate to a manager. At every step, the guest gets an email update about the status of their request.

## Why It Matters

Consistent policy enforcement means every guest gets treated fairly — the refund amount does not depend on which agent happens to answer. Automatic processing of straightforward cases frees guest services staff for complex situations. And the full audit trail means NovaTrek can demonstrate compliance with its published cancellation policies.
