# NTK-10003 - Simple Explanation

## The Problem

When guests arrive at a NovaTrek Adventures base camp, they need to check in before their adventure begins. Guests who have NovaTrek accounts can walk up to a self-service kiosk, sign in, and be checked in within about 3 minutes.

But many guests do not have NovaTrek accounts. These include people who booked through travel partners like ExploreMore or TrailFinder, people who received gift cards, or companions added to someone else's reservation. These "unregistered" guests cannot use the kiosks. Instead, they have to wait in line for a staff member to check them in manually, which takes much longer -- about 22 minutes on average during busy mornings.

This is frustrating for guests, expensive for NovaTrek (extra staff are needed just for this), and it is the number one complaint from partner-booked guests.

## The Solution

We are adding a "Check in with Confirmation Code" option to the kiosk. Unregistered guests will enter four pieces of information to prove their identity:

1. Their last name
2. The reservation confirmation code (from their booking email)
3. The date of their adventure
4. The number of participants on the reservation

If all four match a reservation in the system, the guest gets temporary access to the kiosk to complete their check-in -- including signing any required safety waivers and confirming their gear assignments.

If the system cannot find the reservation directly (which can happen with some partner bookings), it automatically checks with the partner's booking system as a backup.

## Why It Matters

This change lets the majority of unregistered guests skip the staff line entirely. It reduces wait times, lowers staffing costs, and creates a natural opportunity to invite guests to create NovaTrek accounts after their check-in -- turning one-time visitors into returning customers.
